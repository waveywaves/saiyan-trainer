- SeaweedFS for S3-compatible storage (MinIO community binaries dead since 2025 licensing change)

<context>
The project is a complete end-to-end implementation of a neuroevolution fighting game bot with Tekton MLOps orchestration. The strategic goal beyond the demo is discovering what Tekton needs to better support ML/DL workloads, potentially leading to a Tekton ML toolkit.
